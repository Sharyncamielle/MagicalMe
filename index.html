Renowned Artist Sharyn Cartwright Presents "Island Dreams" Collection at The Museum of Modern Art, New York

New York, September 15,2023 - The Museum of Modern Art in New York is thrilled to announce the highly anticipated art exhibition by acclaimed artist Sharyn Cartwright, titled "Island Dreams." This exceptional collection, which Ms. Cartwright spent seven months painstakingly creating, offers a vivid and captivating glimpse into the rich culture and breathtaking beauty of the Bahamas.

Sharyn Cartwright, celebrated for her mastery of color and her ability to evoke powerful emotions through her art, brings the warmth, vibrancy, and unique essence of the island nation to life in her "Island Dreams" collection. The exhibition predominantly features paintings that exude the soul and spirit of the Bahamas, reflecting the artist's deep connection to her homeland.

"We are honored to showcase Sharyn Cartwright's 'Island Dreams' collection at The Museum of Modern Art," said Shanead Claudia, Curator of Contemporary Art at the museum. "Cartwright's work is a testament to her exceptional talent and her ability to capture the essence of the Bahamas in a way that is both visually stunning and emotionally resonant."

Visitors to the exhibition can expect to be transported to the Bahamas through Cartwright's vivid brushstrokes and imaginative compositions. Each artwork tells a story, inviting viewers to explore the captivating landscapes, vibrant traditions, and the soulful rhythm of island life.

"Island Dreams" by Sharyn Cartwright will be on display at The Museum of Modern Art in New York from October 1st to October 29th. This is an exceptional opportunity for art enthusiasts, culture aficionados, and those seeking a visual journey into the heart of the Bahamas. Don't miss the chance to experience this extraordinary collection firsthand.
